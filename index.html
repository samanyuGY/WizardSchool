    SCHOOL OF WIZARDS

    Hello and welcome to the amazing school of wizards.
    Here we teach magic to those who dont know how to control their magic.


    Campus life


    With 500+ student organizations (chocolate science, anyone?), nearly 20 Greek-letter and independent living groups and a commitment to diversity and inclusion, student life at School of Wizards offers a welcoming place for everyone. To complement their academics and research in wizardry, students choose their own extracurricular adventures, from a spectacular array of ways to participate in music, dance, and sports to dozens of groups that savor and celebrate cultures from around the world. To help students navigate challenges, school offers a strong support network (bolstered by occasional visits from puppies, pigs, and pygmy goats).
